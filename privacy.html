Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy details the types of information the **Nazakat Support Bot** ("the Bot") collects, how it is processed, and your rights regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with the Bot, the following data is processed and stored:

  • Discord Account Details: Your Discord User ID, username, and avatar URL are retrieved to identify your ticket and properly display your messages to server staff.
  • Message Content: The text content of messages you send directly to the Bot is logged to facilitate the modmail support system.
  • Attachments: Any images or files you attach to your DMs are re-uploaded to our secure server channels so they can be viewed by the support team.
  • Auto-Moderation Records: If a message is flagged by our keyword filter, the violation count, warning logs, and the flagged word are saved to manage abuse and blacklist enforcement.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To relay your support inquiries to the Nazakat Server Administration and Staff.
  • To allow Nazakat Staff to respond directly to your queries.
  • To check for rate limits and maintain system stability.
  • To moderate and blacklist users who send abusive content or spam.
  • To train the AI auto-responder using in-memory conversation history (conversations are stored in-memory up to a limit of 20 messages and are fully cleared upon ticket closure).

3. Data Storage and Sharing

  • Local Database: All persistent data (blacklist, active tickets, message logs, warning count) is stored locally in an encrypted/secured SQLite database managed on our private hosting server.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third-party entities. The only recipients of your data are the authorized moderators, admins, and developers of the Nazakat Discord Server.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Conversation history and active timers in the bot's memory are cleared instantly when a ticket is closed. Audit logs stored in the database are retained to assist with resolving issues and keeping records of past server promotions or appeals.

If you wish to have your message history deleted from our database, you have the right to request it. Please join the Nazakat server at discord.gg/nazakat and contact our development team to request a manual deletion of your ticket records.
